Horses

Horses are very majestic creatures, they come in all different colours, shapes and sizes. Horses are very sociable animals so they travel in herds that can contain up to 50 horses. The fastest breed of horse is the Thoroughbred and the oldest breed is the Arabian. Arabian horses have a tail shaped like a question mark.

Horses like to eat hay, carrots and apples. A horses digestive system only goes one way, so if a horse eats potatoes or buttercup they will get colic which is food poisoning. If colic isnt treated early they will die. There are over 100 breeds of horses and they all are completely different, not one has the same markings.
